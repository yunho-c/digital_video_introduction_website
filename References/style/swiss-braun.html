02 — Intra-frame

Redundancy.

Spatial compression (JPEG) analyzes a single frame. It identifies blocks of identical or similar pixels and represents them as a mathematical formula rather than individual data points.

Technique: Discrete Cosine Transform (DCT)
Goal: Eliminate high-frequency noise invisible to the human eye.

03 — Inter-frame

Delta Encoding.

The most significant savings come from time. If a pixel does not change from Frame A to Frame B, it is discarded. We only transmit the difference (the delta).
